Point System Rules - Pinatas
What is it?

Campers will build pinatas.

Bouncing balls used as points. Opposite concept like golf. You do not want to have many balls. You want your opposing gender cabin to have more balls so you have a higher chance to earn more from breaking their pinata.

100 balls per age group.

Each cabin has unlimited strikes, but each strike equals one ball owed.

At the end of the week, they will face off during the hitting of pinata. 4 campers from the hitting team and 1 camper from the defending team. They will collect as many balls as they can.

Two wheels - one rewarding and one penalizing. Earn a chance to spin through flagpole. Based on events (decide during NWCCD)

How to collect or lose points/balls

Win nightly challenge versus opposing gender cabin

Memory verses (entire cabin must recide the verse to a DPT member)

Cabins. Will be observed daily starting Monday and ending on Thursday. Most clean and most creative design will be awarded. Messy cabins will be penalized by losing the 'fog of war' by having the fog moved towards them. This means all girls must work together against all boys (and vice versa) to avoid messy cabins.

Games throughout the day will have opportunities for cabins to collect rewards.

Crafts will also have opportunities to collect rewards.

Setting up the tables and clean-up for meal times will earn rewards. Please note that your cabin should be on time and do quality work with a good attitude.

Collecting the most water balloons debris will earn rewards.

Good sportsmanship acts and kind acts will be awarded.

Poor sportsmanship and being late will result in a penalty.

Elementary campers will have opportunities to collect rewards during class time.

Winning

Elementary Girls vs Elementary Boys, Middle School Girls vs Middle School Boys, High School Girls vs High School Boys.

If successful in breaking a pinata open, three members of the hitter's cabin will try to collect as many balls as they can. One member of the pinata being struck can collect balls to prevent the other cabin from collecting balls.

There will be one cabin winner in each age group and the reward will be given before Camp Celebration.
